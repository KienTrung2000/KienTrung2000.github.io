Team Profile

a. Team Name: My current team consists of four people, namely Trung Kien, Huynh Khang, Duc Minh and Anh Quang. Therefore, I decide to choose “4NE1” as my team name. The reason why I choose this name is that we are four separate individuals without knowing each other before. However, when we met, we shared our common goal together, which is bettering ourselves and becoming successful in the prospective future. Especially in the IT field, we are dedicated and willing to explore new things as well as new concepts in order to make the world more modern and innovative with the application of technologies..

b. Team Profile: On my first assignment, I have already done the personality test by using Myers – Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), where users have to answer a questionnaire to see how they perceive the world and make decisions. Thanks to the result, I am able to understand myself more clearly with five different areas, namely mind, energy, nature, tactics and identity so that I can find my appropriate teammates, who will have the same personality with mine or at least we can work together without causing any conflicts. Indeed , I am considered to show a personality type of the Defender, who are tend to be introverted, observant, feeling, judging and turbulent. Moreover, I am efficient and responsible with the ability of paying careful attention to every details in my daily life. In addition, I also took the Learning Style test, which seems to be part of the recruitment process in the future. In particular, the result indicates that I belong to the auditory learner group, who prefer hearing and listening to acquire knowledge. In fact, I find much easier to memorize things by listening carefully and store information in my head rather than noting down and never reading it. Furthermore, I tend to talk or discuss with people when I feel bored because I get interested in hearing what others say about either a specific topic or just a small story.

Hence, according to those two tests, I can finally orient how my upcoming teammates for Assignment 3 will be. Firstly, they should be a good speaker, who are willing to talk and share their opinions with others. In other words, they have the ability of creating a connection among team members with their voice since I lack of this skill, which I am unable to make myself a central of the discussion. Secondly, while I make decisions via feeling, I need someone to resolve problems by thinking. Indeed, according to Norbert Schwarz and Ian Skurnik (2003), it is much better to use your brain instead of your emotion to create a good outcome in some specific cases. In fact, whenever you think carefully or brainstorm ideas before speaking them out, your opinions are more likely to be logical and persuasive, which can thoroughly solve the problems. In contrast, you solve the problem based on your current either positive or negative emotion, which is not certain and may lead to unwise decisions, sometimes. As a result, with a judicious mind, that teammate can help whole team overcome difficulties easily such as misunderstanding or misconception during working together. Thirdly, apart from the personality, it is hard to deny that hobby is also a good factor to help form a good team. Undoubtedly, if we share common hobbies such as playing video games, reading books and so on, we can create a close bond among team members that make the teamwork more comfortable and exciting with high encouragement (Kimberly Zhang, 2020). Thanks to it, everyone will work more productively as well as passionately resulting in optimal outcome.
